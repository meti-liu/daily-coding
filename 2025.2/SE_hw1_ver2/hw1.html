钛狐

“钛狐”是来自南美洲的资深情报顾问。
他的弹道制导系统可以向敌人施压，迫使其做出选择——要么放弃阵地，要么放弃生命。
受到精准打击后，对手往往会失去平衡，被他轻易击败。

Tejo

A veteran intelligence consultant from Colombia,
Tejo's ballistic guidance system pressures the enemy to relinquish their ground - or their lives.
His targeted strikes keep opponents off balance and under his heel.
